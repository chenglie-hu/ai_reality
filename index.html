Welcome to a Space for Growth and Connection

At Kindred Connections, we believe that healthy relationships are the cornerstone of a fulfilling life. Whether you're facing challenges with a partner, family member, or friend, or simply wish to strengthen your emotional bonds, we are here to guide you. Our approach is rooted in empathy, evidence-based practices, and a deep understanding of the complexities of human connection.

We provide a safe, non-judgmental environment where you can explore your feelings, improve communication, and develop the tools necessary to navigate conflicts and build more resilient, loving relationships. Take the first step towards a more connected future today.

Explore Our Services

Our Therapeutic Services

We offer a range of specialized services tailored to your unique relational needs.

Couples Counseling

For partners seeking to resolve conflict, improve communication, and deepen intimacy. We work with couples at all stages of their relationship.

Family Therapy

Address family-wide challenges, improve dynamics between members, and create a more harmonious home environment.

Pre-Marital Counseling

Build a strong foundation for your marriage by discussing expectations, financial planning, and conflict resolution strategies.

Individual Relationship Counseling

Focus on your personal relationship patterns, attachment styles, and how you can contribute to healthier connections in your life.

Secure & Convenient Online Counseling

Receive professional support from the comfort and privacy of your own home. Our secure, HIPAA-compliant video conferencing platform makes it easy to connect with your therapist wherever you are.

Benefits of Online Therapy:
  • Accessibility: Overcome geographical barriers and access specialized care.
  • Convenience: Schedule sessions that fit your busy life without the need for travel.
  • Comfort: Engage in therapy in a space where you feel most comfortable and at ease.

All you need is a stable internet connection and a private space. Get in touch to see if online counseling is the right fit for you.

Useful Information & Resources

What to Expect in Your First Session

Your first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We will discuss what brings you to therapy, your goals, and how we can work together. It's a collaborative process, and your comfort is our priority.

Fees & Insurance

Our standard session fee is $150. We are an out-of-network provider for most PPO insurance plans and can provide you with a superbill to submit for reimbursement. Please check with your insurance provider about your out-of-network mental health benefits.

Confidentiality

Everything you share in therapy is strictly confidential, protected by law and our professional code of ethics. Your privacy is paramount.

About Our Clinic

Kindred Connections was founded on the principle that meaningful relationships are vital to human happiness and well-being. Our lead therapist, Dr. Eleanor Vance, has over 15 years of experience in relationship counseling and is certified in Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and the Gottman Method.

Our mission is to provide compassionate, effective, and evidence-based therapy to help individuals, couples, and families navigate the complexities of their relationships. We are committed to creating a warm and inclusive environment for clients from all backgrounds.
